Chinese Tomato Egg Stir-Fry Recipe

Description

This simple Chinese stir-fry dish is so quick and easy to cook, making it a very convenient meal. It only requires two very basic ingredients: tomatos and eggs. The rest are optional but very important to spice up this dish and contribute to such a savory taste. This dish is usually combined with white rice.

Ingredients

  • 4 small/medium tomatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 scallion
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp white (or black) pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil (optional)
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp vegtable oil
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions

  1. Cut tomatoes into small wedges, finely chop scallions
  2. Crack the eggs in a bowl and season with the salt, pepper, and sesame oil listed above, then beat for a minute
  3. Preheat a pan over medium heat until pan begins lightly smoking. Insert 2 tbsp of vegtable oil then immediately add the eggs. Once the liquid from eggs seem to be solidified, immediately remove the eggs and set aside.
  4. Add 1 more tbsp of oil to the pan, then add the tomatoes and scallions over high heat. Stir-fry for about a minute then add the sugar, salt, and water to the pan. Add the cooked eggs.
  5. Mix everything together, cover the pan for 1-2 minutes until the tomatoes are thoroughly softened.
  6. Uncover and continue to stir-fry until the tomato sauce is to your liking. Serve with rice.
