Chinese Steamed Egg (Easy Version) Using Microwave

Description

This is a very simple dish that goes great with any type of rice, fried or not. I eat it very often and cook it using a microwave for convenience, it's that easy!

Ingredients

  • A microwave (Important)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 scallion, chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil/fish sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium microwave-safe bowl, add egg, water, sesame oil and scallions. Whisk until egg is thoroughly beaten and mixed until fully combined. If your broth is low sodium or unsalted, you can stir in a pinch of salt
  2. Crack the eggs in a bowl and season with the salt, pepper, and sesame oil listed above, then beat for a minute
  3. Cover the bowl with a plate and cook for 4 minutes in the microwave.
  4. Serve and enjoy.
